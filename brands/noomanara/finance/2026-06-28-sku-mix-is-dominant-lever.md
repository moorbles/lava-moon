# SKU mix is the dominant lever and a choice, not a forecast

## Insight
SKU mix is the single biggest sensitivity in the model. It is a strategic choice engineered through merchandising, not a market fact to forecast. The original 60-percent-hero base conflated the acquisition mix with the lifetime mix.

## Why it holds
Cold first orders cluster on the low-commitment entry, the 2-bottle pack, not the £85 box. Hero-heavy is achievable only as a lifetime outcome after people trial and upgrade. Setting the base at 60-percent-hero was setting the base where the numbers look good.

## So what
The live base is 25 single / 45 double / 30 hero, AOV £61.25. The 25 / 15 / 60 hero-heavy mix is the bull and lifetime aspiration, not a cold-acquisition forecast. Engineer the mix through merchandising and the upgrade flow (FIN-005). Do not forecast a mix you have not designed for.

## To validate
The smoke test is built to settle the cold default head-to-head: single vs 2-bottle vs box, measured net of AOV. Highest-priority validation target.
