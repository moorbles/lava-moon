# CPA is the real killer, especially paired with the live mix

## Insight
CPA, driven by creative, is the real risk to the economics. The danger combination is the live mix plus conservative creative: payback slips to about month 11 and year-end Brand 1 cash falls to roughly £4k.

## Why it holds
Mix sets the ceiling, CPA decides whether you reach it. Meta supplement medians run about £43 cost per purchase. The plan's 1.5 percent CTR is optimistic against a ~1.0 percent median, so CPA has downside.

## So what
Do not commit beyond the test batch until both mix and CPA are real numbers from live traffic. Creative volume and quality are the compression mechanism, which is the in-house structural advantage. This is why creative is the central bet, not a decoration.

## To validate
CPA is validated by the live test itself, not the smoke test. Treat it as the dominant residual risk after mix.
