# Starling is the recommended business bank

## Insight
Starling is the strongest fit for Lava Moon: full banking licence with FSCS protection, free monthly fee, native Xero and FreeAgent integrations, and proper multi-director access.

## Why it holds
Multi-director access rules out Mettle despite its otherwise strong feature set, which matters for a two-founder company. The Lloyds £200 incentive is misleading: the fee is £8.50 per month from month 13, and the £200 requires at least £100 card spend by 30 September 2026. Tide matches the £200 and stays free. Revolut is unsuitable now given no meaningful FX need.

## So what
Open with Starling unless the holdco question (LEG-006) changes how many accounts are needed. This banking choice is also a template for Brand 2, so the default is more consequential than it looks. Decision is researched but pending action.
