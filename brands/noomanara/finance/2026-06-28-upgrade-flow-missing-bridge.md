# The post-trial upgrade flow is the missing bridge

## Insight
The honest structure is: acquire 2-bottle-led at roughly breakeven, then migrate customers to the hero box once they have felt benefit, around day 25 to 35. That upgrade flow is the bridge between the £61.25 acquisition AOV and the £68.75 lifetime aspiration. It currently exists in neither the model nor the plan.

## Why it holds
The model holds the acquisition mix for life and so cannot show the migration to the box. Without the upgrade flow, the lifetime AOV is an assertion, not a mechanism.

## So what
Build the upgrade-flow mechanic into both the model and the lifecycle plan. Trigger the upgrade offer at the point of felt benefit in the email flow (CRM-003). This is the next real structural change to the model and one of the hardest parts to execute.

## To validate
Hypothesis until built and measured. Validate by the share of 2-bottle customers who upgrade to the box and the resulting lifetime AOV.
