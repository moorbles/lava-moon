# M6 full-time commitment is a financial trigger, not a date

## Insight
The business does not work at side-hustle bandwidth beyond the test phase. Someone needs to be full-time by month six. The M6 gate is the commitment decision point, triggered by financial performance, not a calendar date or equity ratchet: the business sustaining net profit for two consecutive months sufficient to support both founders at equivalent take-home.

## Why it holds
A soft checkpoint lets a marginal business drift. A financial trigger forces a real decision. If the threshold is met, the second founder commits to full-time within a defined window.

## So what
M6 is a hard commitment gate. It connects to the unresolved resignation-window question if the threshold is not met (LEG-002). Point the AI operating system at Isabel's combined production and ops load first, because she is the bandwidth bottleneck.

## To validate
Locked as the gate. The threshold is read against live P&L.
