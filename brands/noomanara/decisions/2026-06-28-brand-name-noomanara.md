# Brand is Noomanara, with a reusable naming constraint

## Insight
The brand is Noomanara, renamed from Somanara, agreed with Isabel. The root is soma, Greek for body, with soporific literary associations. Field checks found the commercial space clear.

## Why it holds
The rename was a deliberate founder decision. A reusable naming principle emerged: avoid mn consonant clusters and ambiguous double letters, which hurt readability and pronounceability even when linguistically valid. Noomanara was accepted despite the double-o after the readability concern was raised and weighed.

## So what
Noomanara is locked. The naming constraint (no awkward clusters, check domain plus Instagram plus TikTok plus UK IPO Class 5 before committing) is a reusable checklist for Brand 2 naming and a candidate for os-patterns.
