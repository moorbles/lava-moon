# Sleep is the entry product, focus is a future SKU

## Insight
Sleep is the launch product, not focus, even though focus has stronger ingredient science. Focus is a future SKU, not a pivot.

## Why it holds
Sleep wins on emotional salience, the non-melatonin differentiation angle, and the 35 to 55 health-conscious buyer profile. A sleep problem is felt and urgent in a way a focus goal is not, which makes it easier to sell cold. Isabel raised the focus-first challenge and it was considered, not dismissed.

## So what
Brand 1 is the sleep stack. The focus stack (PRD-006) is sequenced behind it. This is the decision the whole launch rests on, so it is recorded as locked with its reasoning intact.
