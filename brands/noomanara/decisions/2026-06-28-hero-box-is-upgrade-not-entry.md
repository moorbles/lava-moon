# The hero box is an upgrade, not an entry

## Insight
The £85 90-day box is the best-value SKU and the lifetime target, reached by upgrade after trial, not pushed to cold traffic. The £85 is the bundle discount itself. Do not stack a further percentage discount on top.

## Why it holds
Cold buyers want low commitment. Pushing the box at acquisition skews the cold mix toward a SKU cold buyers resist, or suppresses conversion. Once a customer has felt benefit around day 25 to 35, the box becomes the natural upgrade at a saving.

## So what
The upgrade is offered via the email flow once benefit is felt. This is the migration the upgrade-flow mechanic must build (FIN-005). Win the trial first, expand commitment when warm.

## To validate
Locked positioning. The upgrade rate is the metric to build and watch.
