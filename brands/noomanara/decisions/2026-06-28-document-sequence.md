# Document sequence is Budget then Media Plan then Playbook

## Insight
Build in order: Budget (done), then Media Plan, then No-Deviation Playbook. Skip the formal business plan. Its content already exists across the overview, media plan, and research, and this is not a fundraise.

## Why it holds
The playbook's core is a change-control rule: deviate only on evidence defined in advance. Its gate criteria should measure operating-system transferability plus a cash-survival check, not Brand 1 P&L alone. The sequence builds the spend plan before the rule that governs deviations from it.

## So what
Media Plan next, with the offer updated to the new SKUs and a PDP and email claims check added. Then the playbook, gate built on the machine-vs-product quadrant (DEC-004) and cash survival.
