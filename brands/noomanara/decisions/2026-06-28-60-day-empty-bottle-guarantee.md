# 60-day empty-bottle guarantee on 2-bottle and 90-day packs only

## Insight
A 60-day empty-bottle money-back guarantee on the 2-bottle and 90-day packs only. The single bottle carries the statutory 14-day cooling-off only.

## Why it holds
A single bottle is 30 days of product. It runs out around day 30, exactly as benefits begin, which would invite a refund at the efficacy trough. A 30-day guarantee was rejected as too short for a product that takes about 30 days to work. A new brand needs more risk reversal, not less. Empty-bottle return limits abuse. Scoping to 2-plus packs also nudges toward the entry pack and lowers claim exposure.

## So what
Locked. The guarantee, the trial, the onset, and the entry SKU all align on the 2-bottle unit (DEC-001).

## To validate
Locked. Watch guarantee-claim rate against the 8 percent return assumption.
