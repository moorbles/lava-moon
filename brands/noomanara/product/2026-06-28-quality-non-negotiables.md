# Quality non-negotiables that gate any supplier

## Insight
Fixed minimum requirements. If a manufacturer cannot confirm all, walk away.

- Mushrooms: fruiting body extract only, not mycelium on grain. Polysaccharide content declared, 30 percent plus for reishi and lion's mane. Triterpene content declared for reishi, 2 percent plus.
- Ashwagandha: KSM-66 specifically, not generic at unspecified withanolide content.
- Manufacturing: GMP certified UK or EU facility, HPMC vegan shell, third-party batch COAs for all actives not generic spec sheets, no artificial fillers beyond vegetable-source flow agents.

## Why it holds
The gap between commodity and genuinely effective product is the difference between a brand that compounds on reviews and one that dies in the first cohort. Fruiting-body-only is also a regulatory protection for lion's mane (REG-001).

## So what
These are the qualifying bar in every supplier conversation. They are also the spine of the transparency positioning and the comparison page (CRE-006).

## To validate
Locked. Confirmation is per-supplier, tracked in supply.
