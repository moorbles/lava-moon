# Capsule format, not powder or gummies

## Insight
Capsules. 2 per serving, 30 servings, 60 capsules, 500mg HPMC vegan shell.

## Why it holds
Powder has shipping complications and a higher customer barrier. Gummies add manufacturing complexity, sugar regulation, and higher MOQ, wrong for a first batch. Capsules are the standard, sourceable from multiple UK manufacturers, and easiest to brand on a small run.

## So what
Locked format for Brand 1. Removes a variable from supplier selection and keeps MOQ and cost in the validation envelope.
