# Sleep stack is four ingredients, magnesium glycinate removed

## Insight
The locked sleep stack is four ingredients: lion's mane, reishi, ashwagandha KSM-66, L-theanine. Magnesium glycinate was removed on dosing-compatibility and differentiation grounds.

## Why it holds
Magnesium at the 2-capsule fill limit lands sub-clinical, and a token dose of a commodity ingredient undermines the credibility positioning. Better cut than included weak. Magnesium is a stronger candidate as a future standalone single-ingredient SKU than as a token inclusion in the stack.

## Drift flag
Several source docs still show the old five-ingredient formula with magnesium glycinate 300mg: Potential_suppliers, Email_to_suppliers_with_ideal_formulations, and noomanara_project_overview.md. These predate the removal. Treat the four-ingredient stack as canonical. The supplier email in particular must be corrected before it goes out, or quotes will come back against the wrong formula. See REG-002 for the claims consequence.
