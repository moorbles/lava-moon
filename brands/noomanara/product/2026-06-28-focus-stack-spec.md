# Focus stack spec (future SKU, scoped not in production)

## Insight
The focus stack is a scoped future SKU, not in production. Five ingredients, two per serving.

- Lion's mane fruiting body extract, 600mg (doubled from the sleep stack to anchor the formula).
- Bacopa monnieri as Synapsa, 300mg.
- Rhodiola rosea, 300mg, 3 percent rosavins, 1 percent salidrosides.
- Citicoline as Cognizin, 250mg.
- L-theanine, 200mg.

COGS roughly £7.50 to £8.50 per unit, suggested retail £38 to £40. Deliberate exclusions: ashwagandha, caffeine, B vitamins, phosphatidylserine.

## Why it holds
Shares lion's mane and L-theanine sourcing with the sleep stack, giving supplier leverage. Focus has stronger ingredient science than sleep, but sleep was chosen as the entry product for emotional salience and differentiation (DEC-008). Focus is the second SKU, not a pivot.

## So what
Hold for post-validation. The supplier qualifying email already covers the focus-specific confirmations (Synapsa, Cognizin, rhodiola ratio) in SUP-004.

## To validate
Validated as a scoped spec. Revisit on launch readiness after Brand 1 PMF.
