# Each ingredient maps to a distinct sleep need-state

## Insight
The four-ingredient stack covers four distinct need-states, which is the basis for framing it as purpose-built sequencing rather than a general blend.

- L-theanine: racing mind and bedtime rumination. The same-night calm (CRM-002).
- Ashwagandha KSM-66: stress and cortisol load.
- Reishi fruiting body: sleep depth and architecture.
- Lion's mane fruiting body: overnight cognitive recovery.

## Why it holds
Different segments enter through different ingredients: anxious-functional via L-theanine, performance male via cognitive recovery, exhausted professional via cortisol and depth. The multi-need-state design is a strength for segmented creative, but broad coverage carries a creative risk: a blend that claims everything reads as generic.

## So what
Frame as purpose-built sequencing, each ingredient doing a named job, not a do-everything blend. This is the product logic that feeds segmented creative (AUD-001) and the formula-as-designed story for the performance male (AUD-003).

## To validate
Validated as formulation logic. The creative framing is testable per segment.
