# Use a what's not in it frame

## Insight
A clear never-contains list communicates purity without making a banned medicinal claim. Fillers, mycelium on grain, silicon dioxide, magnesium stearate. A passive negative sell that raises perceived quality.

## Why it holds
The category is full of cheap additions and mycelium filler. Naming what you exclude signals quality by contrast and is compliance-safe because it makes no efficacy claim. It also reinforces the fruiting-body-only quality story.

## So what
Build a never-contains block into the PDP and comparison page. It does quality-signalling work that claims law forbids you to do directly. Sits alongside the comparison page (CRE-006).

## To validate
Low risk. Watch whether the purity frame lifts add-to-cart on quality-motivated segments.
