# Build a comparison page that does the work

## Insight
A boldly designed us-versus-them comparison converts high-intent comparison shoppers and signals confidence. Compare beta-glucan percentage, extraction method, COA availability, and price per effective dose against named competitors.

## Why it holds
Comparison shoppers are high-intent and close to buying. A page that does the comparison for them removes the last friction and reframes price around effective dose, not sticker price. It only works if the product genuinely wins on the axes shown, which this formula is built to do.

## So what
Build the comparison page once COAs and beta-glucan figures are confirmed from the supplier. Lead with effective dose to defend the mid-premium price. Depends on supply confirmations (SUP-003).

## To validate
Hypothesis until built. Measure conversion rate of traffic that hits the comparison page versus that that does not.
