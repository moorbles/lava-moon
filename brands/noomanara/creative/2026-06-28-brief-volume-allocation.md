# Creative brief volume is weighted by segment value

## Insight
Brief volume is allocated across segments by conversion potential times reachability: Exhausted Professional 28, Perimenopause Processor 27, Performance Male 25, Anxious Functional 20. Launch UGC casting is 3F/1M to match these weights, since Performance Male carries the lowest share.

## Why it holds
Under broad Advantage+ (CRE-008), you cannot target segments, so you reach them by producing creative weighted to where the value is. The allocation reflects who is most likely to convert and most reachable, not equal coverage.

## So what
Brief and cast to these weights. Use a segment-by-angle test matrix to read which combination wins. Pair with the read-signal diagnostics in the media plan to infer which segment is converting under broad targeting.

## To validate
Validated as a planning allocation. Live conversion by inferred segment confirms or re-weights it.
