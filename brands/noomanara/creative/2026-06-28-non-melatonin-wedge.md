# Non-melatonin is the core positioning wedge

## Insight
A meaningful segment of sleep-supplement buyers actively avoids melatonin over dependency concerns and next-day grogginess. Non-melatonin is not a feature. It is the category-entry wedge that selects for this buyer.

## Why it holds
Melatonin is the market default. A real cohort has tried it, disliked the grogginess or worried about dependency, and is looking for an alternative. Leading with non-melatonin speaks to a buyer already in-market and already dissatisfied, a warmer entry than competing on generic better sleep.

## So what
Non-melatonin leads the positioning, not the ingredient list. It frames the felt effect as calm and easier sleep, not sedation (CRM-004), which is consistent rather than contradictory. Compliant under Meta health policy through lifestyle language, not clinical claims. Note the performance male is the exception (AUD-003).

## To validate
Test non-melatonin-led hooks against benefit-led hooks in cold creative. Expect non-melatonin to win on the melatonin-avoidant cohort. Watch CPA at equal CTR.
