# Differentiation is creative execution, not formula uniqueness

## Insight
The claim that no UK brand has this exact formula is false. Competitors exist. Differentiation has to come from creative execution and sourcing quality, not formula uniqueness.

## Why it holds
Named competitors: New Leaf is closest, four of five ingredients in a non-melatonin sleep bundle at £27.49 across three separate bottles. Also fourfive (reishi sleep blend, Informed Sport certified), Aiku, Nutravita, ØTHERS. The formula is not a moat because it is roughly reproducible. What is hard to copy is the creative engine and the sourcing story.

## So what
Never position on formula uniqueness in partner-facing or customer-facing copy. Position on creative, sourcing transparency (the comparison page CRE-006, the what's-not-in-it frame CRE-005), and the single-bottle-versus-three-bottles convenience edge over New Leaf. Verify competitive claims before putting them in any document.
