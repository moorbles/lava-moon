# Anxious-functional UGC converts the exhausted professional

## Insight
UGC produced by the anxious-functional segment converts the exhausted-professional segment. The creator and the buyer are not the same person. Sequence seeding accordingly.

## Why it holds
The anxious-functional creator talks about calm, winding down, a racing mind switching off. That language resonates with the exhausted-professional buyer, who carries mental load and reads the calming framing as relevant without self-identifying as anxious. Producing segment and converting segment differ.

## So what
Seed anxious-functional creators first, then measure conversion across segments, not only within. Brief for the calming-without-sedating angle (CRM-002), the language that carries. Do not assume one creator persona maps to one buyer persona.

## To validate
Tag creative by producing segment once UGC is live. Read which buyer segment converts on each. Confirm or kill the cross-over.
