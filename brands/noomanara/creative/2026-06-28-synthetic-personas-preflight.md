# Synthetic personas preflight creative before spend

## Insight
A panel of eight synthetic customer profiles, two per segment, is used to preflight creative concepts before committing to production or media spend. Each profile carries a sleep trigger in first-person voice, why melatonin failed them, hooks that resonate versus fall flat, format preferences, purchase blockers, and live objections.

## Why it holds
It is a cheap, fast validation layer upstream of real production. It catches obviously weak hooks before they cost anything and surfaces objections to answer in copy. It is not a substitute for live data, but it sharpens what gets made.

## So what
Run new hooks past the synthetic panel before briefing production. This is a method, not a one-off artifact, and a strong candidate to graduate to os-patterns as a brand-agnostic creative-preflight step.

## To validate
Validated as a process. Its predictive value is checked by comparing panel verdicts against live creative performance.
