# Name products after feelings, not fungi

## Insight
Outcome-named products remove the barrier for uninitiated buyers. Sleep or focus is more searchable and emotionally resonant than Lion's Mane Extract 500mg. Keep the mushroom name on pack as credibility, but lead with the outcome everywhere else.

## Why it holds
Most of the market is not mushroom-literate. The outcome is what the buyer searches and feels. The ingredient is proof, not the hook. Outcome-naming also widens the top of funnel beyond people who already know what reishi is.

## So what
Lead PDP, ad copy, and product naming with the outcome. Demote the fungi to the credibility layer on pack and in the ingredient story. Pairs with the non-melatonin wedge as the headline frame.

## To validate
Test outcome-led naming and PDP against ingredient-led on cold traffic.
