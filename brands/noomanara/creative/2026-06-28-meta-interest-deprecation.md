# Meta interest targeting is gone, creative does the targeting

## Insight
Meta deprecated most granular interest categories on 15 January 2026. Run Advantage+ Shopping broad with no interest stacking. Targeting is decided by the algorithm and the creative, not by interest selection.

## Why it holds
With interest categories gone, any plan that relied on interest seeds is not executable. Advantage+ broad lets the algorithm find the buyer. The creative is what selects the audience now: the hook decides who stops and converts.

## So what
Do not write briefs or plans around interest seeds. This is why creative volume and quality is the central lever (FIN-003), not a nice-to-have. Per-segment creative is how you reach segments, since you can no longer target them directly. Older project docs that listed interest seeds are stale on this point.

## To validate
Validated as a platform fact. The implication, creative as the targeting mechanism, is tested by the live creative win rate.
