# L-theanine is the early-signal ingredient

## Insight
L-theanine is the one component a customer can feel the same night. A gentle calm without sedation. It is the bridge that carries the customer through the weeks before the slower mushroom mechanisms accumulate.

## Why it holds
The mushroom actives are cumulative and not perceptible early. A customer with no felt experience in week one is a churn risk before the product has had a chance to work. L-theanine gives an early, real, attributable experience that buys the retention window the rest of the stack needs.

## So what
Lean on the same-night calm in onboarding comms, days 1 to 3. It is a creative and retention angle, not a clinical claim. Calming without sedating is also the core message for the anxious-functional segment, so the angle does double duty (see CRE-003).

## To validate
A/B the day-1 to day-3 flow. Test whether naming the early calm reduces week-one and week-two churn versus onboarding that does not.
