# Performance male enters via single ingredient, not a stack

## Insight
The Performance-Optimising Male is more likely to enter the category through a single-ingredient product, magnesium glycinate or ashwagandha, than through a stack. The stacked formula must be sold as purpose-built and optimised, not generic wellness.

## Why it holds
He frames sleep as an output metric and responds to specificity: dose, mechanism, sourcing standard. A stack reads as generic unless the construction logic is made explicit. Melatonin avoidance is less instinctive here, so the hook is better-quality sleep, not drug-free.

## So what
For this segment, lead creative with the construction rationale and the data, not the non-melatonin wedge. This is the segment where the formula-as-designed story matters most. Note the tension with the locked four-ingredient formula: the single-ingredient entry instinct may favour a future single-ingredient SKU (see product and decisions on magnesium glycinate as a candidate).

## To validate
Test specificity-led creative against outcome-led creative on this segment specifically.
