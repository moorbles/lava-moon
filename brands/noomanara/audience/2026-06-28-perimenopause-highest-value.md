# Perimenopause Processor is the highest-value segment

## Insight
The Perimenopause Processor is often the highest-intent buyer in the category and the most likely to subscribe if the product works. She has reached a pain threshold and is actively researching, not passively scrolling.

## Why it holds
Hormonal sleep disruption is acute and non-negotiable. She will pay for something that reliably helps and responds to education-led content that names the mechanism, not just the outcome. Lion's mane has documented relevance to menopausal brain fog specifically, which gives a credible mechanism story.

## So what
Weight creative and budget toward this segment for subscription LTV, not just first-order volume. Lead with mechanism-named, education-led content. Her likely subscription behaviour is what makes the 2-bottle subscription default land.

## To validate
Confirm subscribe rate and retention by segment once live. Expect this segment to over-index on both.
