# Four primary segments (canonical reference)

## Insight
Four segments drive targeting and creative. Full profiles, triggers, and per-segment hook banks live in the canonical doc and are not duplicated here.

- Exhausted Professional. Women 35 to 50, chronic sleep debt, research-led, deliberately melatonin-avoidant, existing supplement user.
- Perimenopause Processor. Women 42 to 55, hormonal disruption, highest pain threshold, highest subscription likelihood (see AUD-002).
- Performance-Optimising Male. Men 35 to 50, sleep as recovery and cognition, data-aware, enters via single ingredient (see AUD-003).
- Anxious Functional. Any gender 30 to 48, sleep downstream of anxiety, responds to calming without sedating.
