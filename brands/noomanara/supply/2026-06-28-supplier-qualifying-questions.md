# Supplier qualifying questions (reusable artifact)

## Insight
A fixed set of qualifying questions filters manufacturers fast: GMP certificate, fruiting-body-only with declared polysaccharide and triterpene content, KSM-66 specifically, MOQ on custom and closest stock, lead time, UK-compliant label support, third-party batch COAs, and lion's mane Novel Food position with pre-1997 history or FSA correspondence.

## Why it holds
The answers determine fit before any sample is ordered. A thorough response self-selects the serious manufacturers. The bar maps directly to the quality non-negotiables (PRD-002) and the regulatory protections (REG-001).

## So what
