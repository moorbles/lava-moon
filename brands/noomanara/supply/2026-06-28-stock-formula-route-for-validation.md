# Stock-formula route is the validation path

## Insight
Use the stock-formula route to validate: find a manufacturer whose existing formula is close enough and white-label it. Lower MOQ, faster to market, lower cost. Custom formulation is the post-PMF path.

## Why it holds
Custom at 500-plus units pushes inventory toward £3,000-plus and consumes most of the budget before any creative is tested. The point of the 90-day test is to learn whether creative and positioning work, which does not require a bespoke formula. Save custom for after product-market fit is confirmed.

## So what
First conversations ask for the closest existing white-label sleep formula, then custom MOQ as a fallback. Tension parked from the founder call: whether to raise media spend to clear larger stock, or accept the constraint and look at alternative routes. Resolve before committing to a supplier.

## To validate
Locked direction. Open: does a stock formula exist close enough to the four-ingredient spec.
