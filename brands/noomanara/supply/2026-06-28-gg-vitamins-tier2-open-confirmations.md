# G&G Vitamins is a Tier-2 candidate with four open confirmations

## Insight
G&G Vitamins is assessed as a Tier-2 candidate, pending four confirmations before it can be trusted as a supplier.

1. Fruiting body COA with declared actives.
2. KSM-66 sourcing.
3. Third-party batch COAs.
4. Lion's mane UK Novel Food documentation.

## Why it holds
G&G offer an organic mushroom blend available to white label with variable capsule counts and formula alterations, which fits the stock-adjacent route. But none of the quality non-negotiables (PRD-002) or the Novel Food protection (REG-001) are yet confirmed.

## So what
Do not advance G&G past Tier-2 until all four are confirmed in writing. Pursue a costed quote at 500-unit MOQ in parallel. Use the supplier qualifying email (SUP-004), corrected to the four-ingredient formula first (PRD-001).

## To validate
Resolve the four confirmations. Each is a hard gate, not a preference.
