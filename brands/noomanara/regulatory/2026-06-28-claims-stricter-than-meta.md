# Claims law is stricter than Meta policy, design to the stricter standard

## Insight
UK claims law is stricter than Meta's advertising policy. Design all copy to the stricter standard and the Meta requirement is met automatically.

## Why it holds
Meta's January 2025 health update restricts conversion-event targeting and mandates disclaimers, but claims law governs what you can actually say about the product anywhere, owned channels included. Passing Meta review does not make a claim legal.

## So what
Write to claims law, not to Meta policy. Lifestyle and identity language, no clinical or condition claims. Sleep better, not treat insomnia. This applies to PDP and email, not just ads.

## To validate
Validated as the operating standard. The claims review enforces it.
