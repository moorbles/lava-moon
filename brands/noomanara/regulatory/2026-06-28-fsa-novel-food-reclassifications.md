# FSA reclassified Turkey Tail and Cordyceps as Novel Foods

## Insight
The FSA reclassified Turkey Tail and Cordyceps as Novel Foods in late 2025, banning them without authorisation. Lion's mane and reishi remain viable. Take specialist regulatory advice before finalising any species.

## Why it holds
A live regulator action in the exact category. It is the warning shot that species selection carries regulatory risk, not just sourcing risk.

## So what
Stay on lion's mane and reishi for Brand 1. Do not add an at-risk species for differentiation. Any future SKU or Brand 2 mushroom species needs a regulatory check before formulation, not after.

## To validate
Validated. Re-check FSA Novel Food status before any new species decision.
