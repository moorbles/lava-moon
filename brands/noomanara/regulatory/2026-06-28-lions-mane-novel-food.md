# Lion's mane fruiting body permitted, mycelium is unauthorised

## Insight
Lion's mane fruiting-body extract is permitted in GB supplements. Mycelium preparations are unauthorised novel foods. The fruiting-body spec is therefore both a quality choice and a compliance protection. Get supplier confirmation in writing.

## Why it holds
GB Novel Food rules distinguish the two preparations. The FSA's late-2025 reclassification of Turkey Tail and Cordyceps as Novel Foods (REG-004) shows the regulator is active in this category. Lion's mane and reishi remain the safer species.

## So what
Require written confirmation of fruiting-body sourcing and Novel Food position from any supplier before use. This is one of the four open G&G confirmations (SUP-003) and a line in the qualifying email (SUP-004).

## To validate
Validated as the rule. Per-supplier written confirmation is the open action.
