# The SHA must assign the operating-system assets, not just equity

## Insight
If the operating system has value, the SHA must assign the customer list, creative library, research system, supplier relationships, and brand, not just the equity split. These are the assets that transfer to Brand 2 and survive Brand 1 being cut.

## Why it holds
The thesis is that the operating system is the asset and Brand 1 is the vehicle. An SHA that only governs equity in Brand 1 fails to protect the thing the whole project exists to build.

## So what
Add explicit asset assignment to the SHA being formalised via SeedLegals. List, creative library, research system, supplier relationships, brand. This is a defined open task, not yet drafted.

## To validate
Hypothesis until drafted into the SHA. Open task.
