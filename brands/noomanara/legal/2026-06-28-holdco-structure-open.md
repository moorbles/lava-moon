# Open: is Lava Moon a holdco above a separate Noomanara entity

## Insight
Unresolved whether Lava Moon Ltd is a holding entity above a separate Noomanara trading entity, or whether Noomanara is simply a brand operated directly by Lava Moon. This affects how many bank accounts and entities are needed, and how Brand 2 is housed.

## Why it holds
The multi-brand thesis makes entity structure consequential. A holdco with per-brand trading subsidiaries is a different setup from one company running multiple brands. The choice has tax, banking, and asset-assignment implications and interacts with the SHA's OS-asset assignment (LEG-001).

## So what
Resolve the structure before opening banking and before Brand 2. If the OS is the asset, a holdco that owns the OS and licenses it to brand entities may be the cleaner long-term shape. Take advice. Do not let the banking setup harden a structure by default.

## To validate
Open question. Decide holdco-vs-single-entity, ideally with accountant input, before the banking account is opened (FIN-006).
