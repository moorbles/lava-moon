# Partnership failure scenarios are unresolved

## Insight
Three partnership questions are open and need a mechanism in the SHA before they are tested in anger.

1. If Brand 1 fails and one founder wants to continue while the other does not, what is the mechanism: buyout option or clean dissolution.
2. What is the resignation window at M6 if the threshold is not met: notice period or hard stop.
3. What is the deadlock mechanism for decisions requiring joint sign-off.

## Why it holds
These were raised on the founder call and parked. Unresolved failure terms are the expensive kind to leave to goodwill. The M6 gate is a real commitment decision point, so the resignation window matters.

## So what
Resolve all three in the SHA. Action from the call was to review partnership failure scenarios before the next sync. Pairs with the asset assignment (LEG-001) and the vesting audit (LEG-003).

## To validate
Hypothesis until written into the SHA.
